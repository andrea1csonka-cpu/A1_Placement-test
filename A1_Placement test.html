VI. Szövegértés (Reading Comprehension) (20 pont)

Olvassd el a rövid szövegeket és válaszolj a kérdésekre! (Minden kérdés 2 pontot ér)

Text 1: A Busy Weekday

My name is Mark. I live in a small, quiet town with my wife, Emma. I work in an office in the city, so I always wake up early at half past six. I usually eat a quick breakfast of toast and coffee. I take the train to work because driving is slow and expensive. I start work at nine and finish at five. After work, I like to relax. Emma and I often cook dinner together in the kitchen, and then we watch a movie in the living room. We are happy with our life.

81. What is Mark's town like?

82. Why does Mark take the train?

83. What do Mark and Emma do immediately after work?

84. Where do they usually watch a movie?

85. What does Mark usually eat for breakfast?

Text 2: The Green Family

Mr. and Mrs. Green live in a beautiful, old house near a big park. They have two children, a boy and a girl. The boy, Sam, is 10 years old and he loves playing football. The girl, Lily, is younger; she is 6. She often draws pictures of animals. On weekends, the family sometimes goes to the park to have a picnic. They take a basket with sandwiches and fruit juice. They don't like to watch TV a lot, because they prefer outdoor activities. They think spending time together outside is important.

86. What is true about the Green's house?

87. What does Lily like to do?

88. When does the family sometimes go to the park?

89. What do they take for the picnic?

90. What do the Greens prefer to do?
